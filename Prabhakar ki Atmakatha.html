PRABHAKAR RAJ

1. Judaai Ka Har Lamha Kuch Yun Guzaara Hai Humny, Tum Aaogy Tum Aany Waly Ho Tum Aa Jao Shayad.

2. Badal Jao Waqt Ky Saath Ya Phir Waqt Badalana Seekho, Majaburiyon Ko Mat Koso Har Haal Mein Chalana Seekho.

3. Ek Murdy Ny Kiya Khoob Kaha Hai, Ye Log Jo Meri Laash Par Roty Hain, Abhi Uth Jaun To Jeeny Nhi Dengy.

4. Hamdardi Na karo Mujh Sy Aye Hamdard Dosto, Woh Bhi Bary Hamdard Thy Jo Dard Hazaron Dy Gaye.

5. Samet Lo In Naazuk Palon Ko Na Jaany Ye Lamhy Kal Ho Naa Hon, Chaahy Jo Bhi Lamhy Kiya Maloom Shaamil Un Palon Mein Hum Hon Na Hon.

6. Har Jalty Deepak Taly Andhera Hota Hai Har Raat Ky Peechy Ek Savera Hota, Log Darr Jaaty Hain Mussibat Ko Dekh Kar, Har Mussibat Ky Peechy Such Ka Savera Hota Hai.

7. Do Chehry Insaan Kabhi Nhi Bhulata Hai, Ek Mushkil Mein Saath Deny Waala, Dusra Mushkil Mein Saath Chodny Waala.

8. Kisi Ki Yaad Mein Itna Udaas Na Huwa kar, Log Naseeb Sy Milty Hain Udaasiyon Sy Nhi.

9. Sachi Mohabat Kabhi Khatam Nhi Hoti, Bas Waqt Ky Saath Khamoosh Ho Jati Hai.

10. Haisiyat Asman Jaisi Honi Chahiye Kiyun Ki, Zameen Kitni Bhi Unchi Ho Log khareed Hi Lety Hain.

11. Zindagi ko kya Zarurt ha manzilo ki, Waqt har manzil aasan bna deta ha, Marta nhi kissi se judaa ho kr koi, Yeh waqt use bhi jina sikha deta ha.

12. Milegi Parindon Ko Manjil Ye Unake Par Bolate Hain, Rahate Hain Kuch Log Khaamosh Lekin Unake Hunar Bolate Hain.
